Limonada de Lavanda com Cannabis

Limonada floral com xarope de lavanda e cannabis.

Ingredientes
  • Juice of 4 lemons
  • 2 tbsp cannabis lavender syrup
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice
  • Dried lavender
Modo de Preparo
  • Simmer water, sugar and lavender 10 min; strain; add cannabis tincture.
  • Mix lemon juice, syrup and water. Pour over ice.
