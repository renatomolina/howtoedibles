Vegano

Sorvete Vegano de Banana Infusionado

Nice cream de um ingrediente só com óleo de coco de cannabis.

Ingredientes
  • 4 very ripe bananas frozen
  • 2 tbsp cannabis coconut oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • Optional: peanut butter or cocoa
Modo de Preparo
  • Blend frozen banana until smooth and creamy.
  • Add cannabis oil and vanilla; blend again.
  • Add optional mix-ins and pulse.
  • Serve as soft-serve or freeze 1h for scoopable texture.
