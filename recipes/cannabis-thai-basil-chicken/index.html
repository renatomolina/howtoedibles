Cannabis Thai Basil Chicken

Quick Thai basil stir-fry with cannabis sesame oil.

Ingredients
  • 500g ground chicken
  • 2 tbsp cannabis sesame oil
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 bird's eye chillies
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 cup Thai basil
  • Steamed rice
Directions
  • Brown ground chicken in regular oil.
  • Add garlic and chillies. Add sauces and sugar.
  • Off heat: drizzle cannabis sesame oil, toss through basil. Serve over rice.
