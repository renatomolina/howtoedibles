Infused Bulletproof Coffee

Ignite your senses and fuel your day with focus and balance, all in a single cup.

This cannabis infused bulletproof coffee recipe blends infused coconut oil into a creamy, keto-friendly beverage that keeps you in ketosis while delivering a smooth, controlled cannabis experience. Keto cannabis drinks leverage the high-fat ingredients already common in ketogenic diets, like MCT oil, heavy cream, and coconut milk. These are among the simplest and fastest-acting edibles you can make. Cannabis bulletproof coffee blends hot coffee with infused coconut oil and grass-fed butter for a keto-friendly, energizing edible drink.

Ingredients
  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon infused coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Sweetener of choice (optional)
Directions
  • Brew a cup of coffee using your preferred method.
  • In a blender, combine the brewed coffee, unsalted butter, coconut oil, and cannabis-infused coconut oil.
  • Blend on high speed until the mixture is creamy and frothy.
  • Taste and add sweetener if desired.
  • Pour into a mug and enjoy your keto-friendly cannabis-infused bulletproof coffee!

Dosing Tips for Your Infused Bulletproof Coffee

  • Keto drinks with MCT oil may onset faster than typical edibles because MCTs are metabolized more quickly. Start low at 5 mg.
  • Blend thoroughly with a frother or blender to create a creamy emulsion rather than an oily film on top.
  • One drink equals one dose. Do not sip throughout the day without tracking your cumulative intake.
  • Add cannabis fat to hot drinks to help emulsification. Cold keto drinks need a blender to combine properly.
  • Add a measured tablespoon of infused coconut oil to each cup and blend until frothy for even distribution.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Too much MCT oil at once can cause stomach distress. If you are new to MCT, start with half a tablespoon and work up.
  • Not blending the cannabis fat into the drink properly, leaving an oily layer that contains most of the dose.
  • Making a large pitcher without calculating per-cup dosage, which leads to inconsistent servings.
  • Combining cannabis keto drinks with intermittent fasting on an empty stomach can intensify effects dramatically.

How to Store Infused Bulletproof Coffee

  • Cannabis keto beverages are best consumed fresh. Dairy-based versions last 2-3 days refrigerated.
  • Make cannabis MCT oil or butter concentrate and store it, then add fresh to drinks each time.
  • Shake or blend stored keto cannabis drinks before consuming as separation is inevitable.
  • Drink immediately; the emulsion breaks down as it cools and the fats separate.

Variations & Ideas

  • Blend cannabis MCT oil into bulletproof coffee with grass-fed butter for the ultimate keto morning ritual.
  • Mix cannabis coconut cream into matcha latte for an earthy, energizing keto drink.
  • Create a cannabis keto hot chocolate with cocoa, heavy cream, erythritol, and cannabis butter.
  • Add a tablespoon of MCT oil and a dash of cinnamon for enhanced ketone production and warm spice flavor.
